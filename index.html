Welcome to my little site.
The main goal of this is to provide an extensive, but easy to understand resource for all things Vita and hacking it.
It is inspired by the vita.hacks.guide, but I have added and changed some things that I wasn't quite satisfied with on their site.
I am especially trying to make clear why certain things are done the way they are, so users better understand how everything works. Also, pictures!
Sorry if this site looks like ass, but I suck at webdev. It was created using my fifth grade HTML knowledge and referencing w3schools.com.
