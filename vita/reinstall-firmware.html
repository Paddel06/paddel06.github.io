Reinstalling FW on PS Vita: On your PC, do: 1. Download and install Qcma: . 2. Download the firmware you want to reinstall from the `Normal Firmwares` section on . Place the downloaded `PSP2UPDAT.PUP` in Qcma's `PSV Updates` folder in you user's Documents folder (Location may vary per OS). 3. Launch Qcma, open its settings, go to "Other", and change these settings: -Change "Use this version for update" to `Custom`. -Change "Custom PS Vita version" to `0x.xx0.000`, with x.xx being the firmware version you want to reinstall, for example 0**3.65**0.000. Confirm the settings with OK. On your Vita now do the following: 1. Turn off the Vita, then turn it on while holding `PS button + R trigger + Power` to enter Safe Mode. 2. In Safe Mode, select option `5. Update System Software` and then `Update by Connecting to a PC`. 3. Using a USB cable, connect your Vita to the PC running Qcma and follow the onscreen instructions to reinstall your firmware.
